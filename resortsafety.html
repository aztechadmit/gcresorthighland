Safety and Guidelines

Your safety is our number one priority here at the Globe-City Resort Highland. For this reason, many guidelines and security measures are in place throughout the resort.

Notice: All guests must pass through a security checkpoint before entering Adventure Park. All bags will be searched and items not allowed on resort property will be confiscated.

Prohibited Items

The Following Items are Not allowed in Adventure Park, City-Shop, or any other areas on resort property

  • Weaoons and other sharp objects including glass-breakers
  • Class B and above Hazordous Materials or Substances
  • Props, Toy Weapons, and Sports Equipment
  • No outside food or drink unless sealed in original packaging
  • Alcohol, Drugs (including CBD enriched substances or mariguana)
  • Selfie Sticks and Tripods
