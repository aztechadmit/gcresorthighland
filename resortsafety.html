Safety and Guidelines

Your safety is our number one priority here at the Globe-City Resort Highland. For this reason, many guidelines and security measures are in place throughout the resort.

Notice: All guests must pass through a security checkpoint before entering Adventure Park. All bags will be searched and items not allowed on resort property will be confiscated.

Prohibited Items

The Following Items are Not allowed in Adventure Park, City-Shop, or any other areas on resort property

  • Weapons and other sharp objects including glass-breakers
  • Class B and above Hazordous Materials or Substances
  • Props, Toy Weapons, and Sports Equipment
  • No outside food or drink unless sealed in original packaging
  • Alcohol, Drugs (including CBD enriched substances or mariguana)
  • Selfie Sticks and Tripods
  • Paint, graffiti markers, or other marking tools
  • Skateboards, Scooters, Bycicles, Rollerskates/ Rollerblades
    • Note: Bike locks are available at all resort drop-off locations

General Guidelines

  • All guests must adhear to our dress code (see below)
  • Children should not be left unattended
  • All guests must have valid identification and park admission to enter themeparks
  • All guests will be required to pass a security checkpoint before entering the resort Esplenade
  • Guests who are heavily intoxicated will be removed from resort property
  • To preserve immersion, playing of loud music or video in guest areas is prohibited

Security and Medical Emergencies

  • Report all security concerns to the nearest cast member
  • Unattended children will be directed to the Child Care Center in the Resort Esplenade
  • If a guest has lost their group, they can provide a note at Guest Services
  • On-site medical personal are available to assist in any medical emergencies
  • A first-aid office on Adventure Boulevard is available for non-emergency medical needs
  • Click here for more information on lost group members or children
