What is Season of Light?

Globe-City's Season of Light Celebration is an annual event that takes over the entire resort. This event is included with general admission and features exclusive attractions including in-park decorations, new shows and entertainment, and exclusive rides and experiences. When is Season of Light?

This event occurs annually from the first Friday of November until the end of the first week of January. Although decorations will begin taking shape November 1st, all entertainment and exclusive event offerings will not be available until the event officially begins.
