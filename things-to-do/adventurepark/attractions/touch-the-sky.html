Soar to New Heights

Touch The Sky is a unique rollercoaster experience with a 60 mph launch and 2 inversions including a 30 foot loop over Adventure Lake! Prepare for airtime and the rush of an 80ft drop.

Music for the Thrills

In addition to physical thrills, prepare to get your heart thumping to the beat of our unique original soundtrack, performed by the Oras Nou Harmonic Orchestra, which brings together the classic and timeless sounds of golden-age music with the fast-paced thrill of an areal score.

Know Before You Go

Touch the Sky is an intense launched rollercoaster with accelerations of up to 16m/s and high levels of G-force. Due to the nature of this ride, it is advised that expectant mothers and guests with recent medical history do not ride. Please be advised that due to the restricting nature of this attraction's restraints, some guests may be unable to ride.
