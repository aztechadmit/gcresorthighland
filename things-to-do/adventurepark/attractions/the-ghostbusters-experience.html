Know Before You Go

Touch the Sky is an intense launched rollercoaster with accelerations of up to 16m/s and high levels of G-force. Due to the nature of this ride, it is advised that expectant mothers and guests with recent medical history do not ride. Please be advised that due to the restricting nature of this attraction's restraints, some guests may be unable to ride.
