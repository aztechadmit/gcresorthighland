The Magic Bakery Ride

Journey into the land of oversized magic sweets and treats on this tour of the famous magical Bakery from Hindsborg Village.

Attraction Type: Indoor Dark Ride

Attraction Tags: Family Friendly, Dark Ride, Magical, Musical

Location: Hindsborg, Adventure Park

Attraction Duration: 4 minutes

Minimum Height: None

Queuing: Priority Queuing available

Accessibility: Guest must transfer from wheelchair ›

Explore the Magical World of Sweets and Treats

On this one of a kind ride, the mystical witch of the Hindsborg Bakery brings you on a tour of the magical world of candy, desserts, and fantasy that fuels her tasty and fulfilling recipes. Watch out, as you may just encounter the mystical wild life while on your journey!

This ride is fit with a great level of detail and care that shows in its stunning display of color, effects, and sounds. The mystical soundtrack performed by the University of Minecraftia orchestra only adds to the unforgettable and dreamlike experience and is sure to open you up to a whole new world of magic.
