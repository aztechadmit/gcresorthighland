Sally's Famous Mansion

For over 100 years, the once magnificent mansion of Sally Spookson has sat vacant at the end of the lively boardwalk. Known for her extravagant parties and strange past, Sally Spookson was the talk of the town with only the most elite of guests attending her functions. Today, the Adventures and More company has bought the mansion and opened the long-abandonded structure for tours.

Rumors and Stories

Although evidence is minimal, many tourists have reported accounts of strange and haunting experiences within the mansions walls, leading some to believe it may posess more than just cobwebs and rotting furniture...

In 2005, famed Ghost-Hunter Jackson Hauntwauld toured the mansion for himself and discovered something very creepy... after his tour, reports say Hauntwald went insane and vanished from society. Before leaving, he wrote a letter to the company offering the tours. In his letter, Hauntwauld warned that those who enter the mansion never leave quite the same.

But this is just a myth... or is it? Find out for yourself when you take the tour of Sally's Manor.
