Take a Tour of the Busiest Spot in the Galaxy

Board a sleek space-car and take a relaxing ride around Cosmic Center learning all about the futuristic Universal Trading Post. On your tour you will pass through the Cosmic Center Institute of Interspace where you will take a look inside the important research being conducted inside.

View Cosmic Center From Above

Cosmic Center is an interplanetary trading post located at the center of the starship galaxy. While on the Galactic Starway, you will have the opportunity to view this cojoining of planets from above on an elevated track.
