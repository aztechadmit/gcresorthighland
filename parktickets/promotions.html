Offers and Discounts


Oras Nou Resident Admission

Oras Nou residents can save up to $30 per ticket on standard admission to Adventure Park. Simply select "Oras Nou Resident Admission" when purchasing tickets.




Discounted Retail Multi-Day Tickets

During the Spring season, 3-Day Discounted Admission packages will be available at most large-scale retailers in Minecraftia. For a list of participating retailers and more information, visit the Multi-Day Retail Admission page.




RMember In-Park Discounts

RMembers with valid RMember IDs or associated emails can receive up to 12% discounts at in-park restaurants and retail stores. Simply show your RMember ID or provide the email address associated with your membership at checkout.




RMember Discounted Annual Passports

RMembers purchasing or renewing their Resident or Adventure Annual Passports during the months of January through May can recieve a 12% discount on its cost. To apply the discount, order your passports through your RMember portal.

Globe-City Resort employees can find employee exclusive discounts through the Globe-City Employee Portal

For Groups of 10 or more, submit a request for discounted admission.
