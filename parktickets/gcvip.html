VIP Perks and Benefits

Priority Queuing

Scan your ticket at any GC-VIP queue entrance within applicable attractions. Depending on your ticket, you may have one or more uses at each priority queue. Once your ticket is scanned and approved, you may enter the priority queue for a shorter wait-time than posted at the attraction's standby entrance.

  • Touch the Sky
  • Sally's Manor
  • Ride the Beat: Waveriders
  • The Ghostbusters Experience
  • Tomb of the Emperor's Curse
  • Crusader
  • Flight of the Dragon
  • The Magic Bakery Ride
  • Old Yander's Mine Train
  • Mission 12
  • The Galactic Starway
  • Journey to Atlantis

    • Please note, priority queues at some attractions may close in some instances.


VIP Lounge Spaces

Our exclusive VIP lounges feature comfortable indoor seating away from theme park crowds. Vending machines, water refill stations, and complimentary WIFI are also available. GC-VIP guests with Gold and Deluxe tickets can access our VIP lounges by scanning their tickets at the entrance. Lounge space is first-come first-serve and some lounges may be closed or unavailable during your visit. To find VIP lounges, use a park guide map or the GC RESORT mobile app.

Priority Viewing Areas

For our live entertainment and nighttime spectaculars, GC-VIP guests with Gold and Deluxe tickets can enter our priority seating areas for a better view of the show without the hassle of reserving a seat. Priority seating areas open 1-hour before showtime and seating is first-come first-serve.

Prefered Parking

Although GC-VIP day tickets do not include free parking, showing a GC-VIP ticket at the parking booth will grant guests discounted general parking or discounted preferred parking. Guests with VIP Deluxe Annual Passports get free general parking in Adventure Lot.

About Priority Parking ↓

Prefered parking is a section of the Adventure Parking lot closest to the Esplenade. Guests who pay for prefered parking will be directed to this section and can skip the long walk from general parking to the esplenade.



Frequently Asked Questions

If I have general admission, can I still purchase GC VIP perks? ↓

Yes, while you are in the park, visit a VIP Kiosk to upgrade your general tickets to GC VIP privileges. Your price will be calculated based on the VIP tier you choose and the day.

What if I have the Priority Bronze only ticket and want to go on a ride more than once? ↓

Although the Bronze Tier Ticket is limited to one use per ride, you can purchase additional uses for an extra charge at any VIP Kiosk. You can also utilize the General Queue or Single Rider's Queue for no additional charge.
