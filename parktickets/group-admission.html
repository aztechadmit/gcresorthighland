About Group Admission

Group Admission is designed to provide the best price as well as ease of use to large groups visiting the themepark. Currently, when using our standard ticketing portal, a maximum of 9 tickets can be purchased at one time. For groups larger than 9, please use the form below to order your tickets.

How to Order Group Tickets

To order group tickets, fill out the form below or contact or Group Sales office at the following contact information:
    Globe-City Resort Group Sales
    (312) 872 5331
    groupsales.gcrh@gcpr.net

What is the Discount for Large Group Admission?

The admission discount for large groups is calculated based on the date selected for your group's visit as well as the number of tickets purchased. Discounts range from 2% to 20% off the regular cost of admission.

How Does Group Admission Work?

First, order your tickets through our Group Sales office or through the form below. Then, someone will need to pick up your tickets either the day-of or day before your visit by visiting the ticket booths in the Resort Esplenade. The ticket booths will print your tickets which can then be given to your group members to enter the themempark on the day of your visit.

Rules and Restrictions

Please be advised of the following restrictions on group sales:
  • Group Tickets cannot be refunded, transfered, or sold
  • Group Admission does not include parking
  • GC-VIP admission is not available for groups larger than 9
  • Discounts, promotions, or other admission savings cannot be applied to group ticket purchases
  • Group Sales are limited to 999 guests
      • For larger events, please contact Group Sales directly
