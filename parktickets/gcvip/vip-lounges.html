Globe-City VIP Lounges

Take your themepark experience to a higher class with exclusive indoor lounge spaces throughout Adventure Park, available only to GC-VIP gold and deluxe guests.

Sasson Family Lounge

Located in the Main Entrance Plaza near Guest Services

A classy victorian style lounge themed after the golden age of Hollywood with a complimentary coffee bar and snack stand



Cosmic Starlight Lounge

Located in Cosmic Center

A futuristic lounge with views of the cosmic center universe and docking station featuring complimentary coffee, drinks, and light snacks.



The Magic Bakery Lounge

Located in Hindsborg Village

Take a small set of stairs up to the second story of the classy french plaza atop the Magic Bakery ride for a quaint sit in a classy lounge with views of the queue on one side and views of the magical forest on the other.
