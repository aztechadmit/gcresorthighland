Globe-City's Adventure Park

Annual Passports

Don't Limit Your Adventure

With a resort annual passport, expand your visit to over 280 days of the year at no additional charge!

Our passports are offered in three tiers for maximum flexibility and a price that matches your budget. All passports are valid for 12 months following the date of your first visit1


Passport Tiers

Resident Passport

  • Admission to Adventure Park on over 2802days
  • 5% Discount on food and drink in-park only
  • Passholder exclusive pricing for parking in Adventure Parking Lot
Starting at $155

Adventure Passport

  • Admission to Adventure Park on over 3152days
  • Free general parking in Adventure Parking Lot4
  • 5% discount on food and drink, 10% discount on merchandise in-park only
  • Up to 8% discount on food and drink at participating retailers in City-Shop
Starting at $205

Platinum Passport

  • Unlimited admission to Adventure Park (365 days of the year!)
  • Free general parking in Adventure Parking Lot4
  • 11% Discount on food and Drink, 15% discount on merchandise in-park only
  • Up to 10% discount on food and drink at participating retailers in City-Shop
  • Complimenary "Bring a Friend" ticket with each renewal of a platinum passport5
Starting at $399


Each annual passport admits one guest to adventure park. Only the guest named on the passport will be permited to enter. Biometric reading will be required to use the passport at the park entrance.

Bring-A-Friend does not include GC-VIP admission. Rules and restrictions apply.

On these days however, passholders can purchase discounted general admission through the passholder ticketing portal or by visiting the ticket booths in the Resort Esplenade.
