Resort First-Aid Services

For your convenience and comfort, there are two first-aid locations within the resort, one in Adventure Park and one in City-Shop.

Our First-Aid Sations are sponsored by Aztech Medicine

Medical Services

Our first-aid stations are staffed with trained medical personel at all times during resort operating hours

    Standard Operating Hours: 10AM - 11PM


Convenience Items

Our First-aid locations are equipped with the following free-of-charge items:
  • Ibuprofen and Acetaminophen - 200mg
      Ibuprogen and Acetaminophen cannot be taken together nor within 6 hours of one another
  • Neausea Medication
  • Generic non-drowzy allergy medicine (compare to Zertec) - 10mg
  • Pads and Tampons
  • Bandages, disinfectant, and gauze
  • Cold water and Hyrdation Multipliers
  • Blood Sugar Tablets
  • Beds and Sitting Area

Disclaimer: Due to federal legislation, first-aid cannot offer more than the legal dosage of medications to any guests regardless of circumstance.


Locations

First-aid stations are located in the following places throughout the resort:
  • Adventure Boulevard (Across from the Journey to Atlantis Entrance)
  • City-Shop North (Adjacent to the Fountain Plaza)
Please ask any cast member for directions to first-aid stations if necessary.
