Esplenade Child Care Center

For families traveling with young infants, a Child Care Center is available in the Resort Esplenade with support from trained child-care proffesionals. Additionally, children under the age of 12 who are lost or separated from their party will be escorted by resort security to the child care center until an adult returns for them

Operating Hours

The child-care center is open 30 minutes before the earliest park opening to 1 hour after the park has closed.
    Per Oras Nou legislation, children who have not been picked up past 1 hour after park closing will be handed to the Oras Nou Police Department's Child Abandonment Service - (300) 122 2100.

Services

The child-care center offers paid babysitting during standard operating hours.
    Baby sitting will cost $250 for the whole day or $60 for the hour
In addition to baby sitting services, the Child Care Center also features the following ammenities
  • Nursing room with chairs
  • Changing room with tables
  • Toddler-size toilets (for Guests under 42” tall)
  • Feeding area with highchairs
  • Kitchen with sink, bottle warmers and microwave (select use only)
  • Vending machine offering formula, baby food, juice, diapers, wipes, sunscreen, pacifiers and over-the-counter medication—all available for purchase with a credit card

Locations

The child care center is located in the Resort Esplenade adjacent to the Will-Call booth (right of the Adventure Park turnstiles)
