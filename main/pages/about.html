About the Resort

The Globe-City Resort Highland is a place where adventures take place. Featuring a 57 acre themepark, two resort hotels, and a modern shopping center, we have everything you need for days full of fun.

In The Begining

The Globe-City Resort opened on May 26th 2016 to a crowd of 18 thousand excited visitors. Originally, guests visiting would find a small themepark with 12 attractions, a lake-view hotel, and a parking lot across the plaza. Today we have expanded our little park to now feature over 36 attractions and entertainment offerings, an entire shopping center, and a deluxe resort hotel. And although we may have grown in scale, our central idea remanins: create Adventures and Magic for everyone who steps through our gates.



Our Mission

Since the beginning, our focus has been completely on you, our guests, and the memories you make at our resort. Our talented team has perfectly curated timeless attractions designed to immerse, entertain, and fascinate adventurers of all ages.

We pride ourselves on our guest-first company policy that puts all profits and business aside allowing your experience to take the forefront and emphasize the most important asset in our park: you!



More Than Rollercoasters

Globe-City Parks and Resorts is unique in how we place our focus on the adventure beyond the rollercoasters. To us a theme park is much more than its rides, its about the story, the immersion, and the details that make the real world fade away and the worlds of fantasy, future, and fiction come to life. It's why our creative team spends countless time curating unique entertainment, character interactions, and music that complements every aspect of Adventure Park.



Impeccable Safety Standards

In addition to exciting experiences, we take pride in our strict and impeccable safety standards. Despite providing you a fun adventure for all, your safety is ultimately our #1 priority. Our rides are inspected multiple times a day and our maintenance team works around the clock to ensure everything operates safely and effectively. Outside of attractions, we have a talented team of resort security and operations personnel to ensure every corner of the resort is safe, secure, and full of fun!
