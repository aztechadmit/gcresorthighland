Event Venues

at the Globe-City Resort Highland

Classy venues able to host all sorts of events and confrences to make your event stand out

The Boulevard Theater

Located inside Adventure Park, the Boulevard Theatre is the ultimate venue for your event. Guests may access the venue from either a private entrance on Adventure Boulevard (additional fee) or from the backside of Adventure Boulevard through a private gate on the Entrance Plaza.



CityView Terrace

Located atop the City-Shop Terrace, this private venue offers stunning views of City-Shop and a romantic private terrace with ample space. The City-Shop Terrace offers a comfortable mix of indoor vaulted ceilings and outdoor patio space for a beautiful event.



Lakeside Banquet Hall

Located in the East Wing of the Lakeside Hotel, this venue holds great prestige and offers a flexible space for any event. Vaulted ceilings and modern architecture makes for a beautiful set to make your event stand out

Jacob Painter Confrence Center

Located in City-Shop, this multi-story confrence center features a multitude of classrooms and mixed-use event halls for a large flexible venue to hold your corporate confrences or training events.



The Boulevard Diner

Rent out the Boulevard Diner inside Adventure Park for a private breakfast or brunch event. Limited to two hours, themepark admission is included in the per-head price of renting.
