Single Riders Queuing

Cut down your waittimes with the Adventure Park single riders queues

How it Works

The Single Riders queue is used to fill empty seats in attractions. Single Riders will not be able to ride with their party as they will be split apart to fill any remaining seats once the priority and standby queues have been placed onto a vehicle.

Who Can Use Single Riders?

Single riders is free to use for any guests who meet the minimum height requirement for riding alone

note: Although children may use the single riders queue so long as they meet the height requirment, it is strongly recomended that parents wait in line with their children and then exit once their child has boarded the attraction.

Which Attractions Have Single Riders Queues?

Please note, although these attractions feature single riders queues, the queue will close periodically depending on the capacity of the queue.
  • Ride the Beat: Waveriders
  • Touch the Sky
  • Crusader
  • Mission 12
