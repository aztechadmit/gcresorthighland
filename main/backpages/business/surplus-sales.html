Commercial Surplus Sales

As part of the R and R IndustriesTM department of Surplus Sales Management, the Globe-City Resort Highland offers select surplus items for sale. The R and R Industries Surplus Store serves as an outlet to re-utilize, liquidate, recycle and dispose of excess and decommissioned company property. Surplus Sales also offers recycling services to subsidiaries for various metals, electronics, video monitors, and refrigeration equipment.

How To Shop

To shop our surplused items, visit the R and R Industries surplus sales website at:
    www.surplus-sales.rr.net


About Surplus Sales

Surplus Sales helps us reuse and recycle extraneous items that would otherwise have no use. The money earned from selling these items is funneled back to the department that provided the items (minus minor costs of logistics).
Items available from the Globe-City Resort include audio/video equipment, office supplies, facilities maintenence supplies, commercial construction supplies and hardware, and more.

For Resort Departments

To Send excess materials to surplus sales, contact your Interior Logistics department manager. For IL managers, contact the Globe-City Entertainment LLC. surplus manager below to schedule item inventory and pick up by R and R Industries Surplus Management.

R and R Industries Surplus Management

The SM team at R and R Industries focuses on the movement and sale of surplus items from all the subsidiaries of the company. The SM team does not collect profits from surplus sales minus the cost of transporting and holding the items to be sold. Surplus Management is one of the many ways R and R Industries helps to support a renewable and environmentally friendly economy.
